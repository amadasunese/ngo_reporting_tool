About

The Test Score Analyzer helps quantify knowledge gain from training interventions using pre-test and post-test scores.

What it does

  • Calculates mean pre-test and post-test scores.
  • Computes average gain and percentage improvement.
  • Optionally disaggregates results by gender.
  • Generates DOCX and PDF reports for donor-style reporting.
